About Us

At Acuris we're working on an orchard management system that will provide analytics, disease detection and automation solution for kiwifruit growers. At the moment we are focusing on accurate fruit counting using photogrammetry and neural networks.

Utilising the recent advancements in artificial intelligence and machine learning, we aim to analyse crop, detect disease and forecast yield. Beyond just analysis we want to develop a solution that will robotically automate the kiwifruit growing processes, including the culling of flowers, spraying of pesticide and picking of the fruit itself.
