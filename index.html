WHO WE ARE

Orchardists currently rely on manual crop counting, using a tiny sample size to guess valuable crop loads.

Acuris gives orchardists a more comprehensive and reliable way to monitor their crop. Our robots navigate, photograph and intelligently annotate every block - completely automatically.

Version 4. Our current prototype.

ACCURATE

Acuris industry leading software not only provides greater accuracy than normally possible, but also samples an entire orchard, providing a comprehensive ...

INTUITIVE

Explore your orchard from your office: Acuris’ easy-to-use map interface allows users to examine details down to individual detections, bay by bay.

Automatic

Acuris robots are set and forget, automatically navigating orchards and processing the data for easy use. A coordinated fleet can be run without issue by a single user.
